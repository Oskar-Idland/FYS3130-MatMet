\]
\[
\lim_{n \to ∞} \left|\frac{3^{n}(z+4-2i)^{2n + 2}}{3^{n+1}(z+4-2i)^{2n}}\right| = \lim_{n \to ∞} \left|\frac{(z+4-2i)^2}{3}\right| < 1
\]
\[
\lim_{n \to ∞} \left|z + 4 - 2i\right| < \sqrt{3}
\]
